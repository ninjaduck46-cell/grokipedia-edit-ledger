# Biden: AUKUS Pact & NATO Expansion

**Suggested Addition (Presidency → Foreign Policy)**

> Biden initiated the **AUKUS pact** (2021) for nuclear-powered submarines to Australia. NATO expanded with Finland and Sweden following Russia’s invasion of Ukraine. U.S. global approval rose to 46% by 2023.
