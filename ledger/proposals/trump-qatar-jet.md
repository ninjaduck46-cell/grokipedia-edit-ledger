# Trump: Qatar Boeing 747-8 Gift

**Suggested Addition (Post-Presidency / Ethics)**

> In February 2025, Trump accepted a $400 million Boeing 747-8 from Qatar as a presidential gift, rebranded as “Trump Force One.” The move raised ethics concerns under the Foreign Emoluments Clause.
