# Biden: Inflation Reduction Act & Clean Energy

**Suggested Addition (Presidency → Climate Policy)**

> The **Inflation Reduction Act (2022)** invested $369 billion in clean energy, spurring a 17% increase in U.S. renewable capacity in 2023 — the largest climate investment in U.S. history.
