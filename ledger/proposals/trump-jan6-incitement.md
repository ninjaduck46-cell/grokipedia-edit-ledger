# Trump: January 6 Speech & Incitement

**Suggested Addition (2020 Election / January 6 section)**

> Trump’s January 6, 2021, speech included calls to “fight like hell” and march to the Capitol. The House January 6 Committee cited it as contributing to the riot (5 deaths, 174 arrests). He was impeached for incitement of insurrection and acquitted by the Senate.
